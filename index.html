Timekeeper and Logs

Track Your Time and Manage Requests

The Timekeeper system allows you to clock in/out, view work hours, submit time-off requests, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I request for log?

Open your kiosk. Go to Apply "Time log Requests", then fill out the form if it's for a specific log or a whole day logs.

I accidentally uninstalled the timekeeper app and I forgot my password to sign in. What should I do?

Go to web kiosk and try signing in with the password you've set. If still cannot sign in, then proceed to reset your password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link. After you've reset your password, try to sign in again in the timekeeper app.

I cannot proceed to time in/out due to an error that says contact administrator. What should I do?

Try out first to download data and update device settings. If the error persists, try to uninstall and install the app again. Just make sure all your logs are synced before uninstalling the app.

I cannot proceed to time in/out because of invalid location. What should I do?

Please message ARSS and send the Location name and its coordinates - these are the numbers displayed in your mobile screen upon taking the selfie. Once HR has confirmed, kindly click on "update device settings" in the timekeeper app.

I forgot to time in/out?

Is this your first time?

If yes, kindly email HR with the Subject "CODE_First TimeForgot to Time In/Out" with the content of what happened, how it happened and what's your action plans to avoid this from happening.

If not your first time, that will be considered as "Incomplete/No logs, No Pay". Unless with very valid and compelling reason, you should send HR a documentation of it so you can proceed with the request log. This will be subject for Ma'am Juvy's approval, before your manager will accept the requested log.

I cannot apply for Vacation/Sick Leave due to an error. What shoudl I do?

Please try applying using the Web kiosk. If the error persist, contact HR.

SSS/HDMF/PHIC and YAHSHUA Applications

Apply for and Manage Employee applications

The Applications system allows you to apply for employee loans, leaves, and others and manage your existing loan accounts.

Frequently Asked Questions

I applied to SSS online for a Salary loan. What should I do to have it certified by YAHSHUA?

Please send an email to HR and CC Ma'am Juvy stating that you have applied for SSS salary loan and it's subject for employer's certification.

I already received my SSS Salary Loan. How can I have it deducted to my payroll?

Please apply for a Cash Advance through your kiosk. Select the regular deduction and make sure you choose the "SSS Salary Loan" type. The total amount should be the monthly amortization multiplied by how many months your loan is for.

I have requested for Cash advance for my SSS Salary loan. What should I do next?

In the same email thread of your SSS salary loan, notify HR that you have submitted your Cash Advance request and it's subject for approval.

I applied for HDMF loan online. What should I do next to have it deducted to my payroll?

Please apply for a Cash Advance through your kiosk. Select the regular deduction and make sure you choose the "HDMF Loan" type. The total amount should be the monthly amortization multiplied by how many months your loan is for. Also the start date should be based on our cut off period and should be 1 month before the scheduled monthly amortization scheduled by Pag-ibig

My loan application is requiring me for COE with compensation. What should I do?

Please send an email to HR and CC Ms. A and Ma'am Juvy stating your request to process the COE with compensation for what purpose.

My loan application is requiring me for COE without compensation. What should I do?

Please send an email to HR and CC Ms. A and Ma'am Juvy stating your request to process the COE with compensation for what purpose.

My loan application is requiring me for payslip with signature of authorized representative What should I do?

Please send an email to HR and CC Ms. A and Ma'am Juvy stating your request for payslip with signature of authorized representative.

I applied for loan in a bank and it's asking for HR's number for employment verification. What number should I give?

You may give HR's number, 0917 314 3156. And please notify ARSS that you have applied for loan and it's subject for verification.

Am I required to submit medical report during my Sick Leave?

Yes. Please attach the medical certificate upon request for Sick Leave via kiosk or timekeeper app applications module.

My SSS number is currently on temporary Status. What should I do to update it into permanent?

You may click on this link for online guide https://youtu.be/sFx_-q8SEBM. If you cannot proceed, go to the nearest SSS office file your request.

I applied for reloan in SSS/Pag-ibig. What should I do with my current deduction in payroll?

Please send an email to HR for your loan to be certified. Once loan amount is received, proceed to booking your reloan amount details via Kiosk. If you forgot how to do it, just go to "I already received my SSS Salary Loan. How can I have it deducted to my payroll?" FAQ.

I am required to submit MDR to PhilHealth office. Where can I access it?

Just send an email to ofp@philhealth.gov.ph together with your complete name (last name, first name, middle name), your date of birth, and present address. You will then receive a notification with a copy of your official MDR.

I will be taking maternity leave soon. What should I submit before taking the leave?

Please send to HR the following documents at least 3 months before your due: Ultrasound report, Filled out SSS Maternity Leave Notification Form and copy of (1) valid ID.

I just delivered my baby. What should I comply for the SSS Maternity Reimbursement?

Please send to HR the Official Receipt or Acknowledgement receipt from PSA or the Local Civil Registrar along with your baby's birth certificate.

Frequently Asked Questions

What's our regular fitness schedule?

Regular fitness session is every Tuesday at 5-5:30 Pm and Friday at 4-4:30 PM.

If ever I got sick, do I need to report to HR and provide a medical certificate?

Yes! Aside from notifying your manager, you are also required to submit a Medical Certificate from your doctor to HR via email.

What is the best way to start a fitness routine?

Start with realistic and achievable goals. Incorporate a mix of cardiovascular exercises, strength training, and flexibility exercises. Begin with small steps, like 30 minutes of activity three times a week, and gradually increase the intensity and frequency.

How often should I work out to see results?

Aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous activity per week, combined with strength training exercises at least twice a week for best results.

What exercises should I focus on for overall fitness?

A well-rounded fitness routine includes cardio exercises (like running, swimming, or cycling), strength training (using weights or bodyweight), and flexibility training (like stretching or yoga). It's important to balance all three for overall health.

How long will it take to see progress?

Results vary depending on your starting point, but most people begin to notice changes within 4-6 weeks with consistent effort. Visible changes may take a little longer, but improvement in strength, endurance, and energy can be felt early on.

What should I eat to support my fitness goals?

Focus on a balanced diet rich in lean proteins, whole grains, fruits, and vegetables. Protein helps build muscle, while healthy carbs provide energy. Stay hydrated and consider small, frequent meals to keep your energy levels steady.

How can I stay motivated to keep working out?

Set clear goals and track your progress. Find a workout buddy, join group classes, or participate in company fitness sessions for accountability. Celebrate small wins and switch up your routine to keep things interesting.

Is strength training important for weight loss?

Yes, strength training helps build muscle, which increases your resting metabolism, helping you burn more calories even when you're not exercising. Combining strength training with cardio is effective for weight loss and toning.

Can I work out if I have a busy schedule?

Yes, even short bursts of activity can make a difference. Try 15-20 minute high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workouts, or break your workout into smaller segments throughout the day. Consistency matters more than duration.

How important is rest and recovery?

Rest and recovery are crucial to prevent injury and allow your muscles to repair and grow. Ensure you get enough sleep and take rest days between intense workouts to avoid burnout.

What's the role of stretching in fitness?

Stretching improves flexibility, reduces the risk of injury, and helps your muscles recover faster. Incorporate stretching before and after workouts to enhance mobility and prevent soreness.

Our payroll is released every 15th and 30th/31st of the month. Our payroll cutoff is every 21st (of previous month) to 5th (of the current month) for the First Quincena, and every 6th to 20th (of the current month) for the Second Quincena.

What's our process in giving disciplinary actions to YAHSHUAns?

Our disciplinary actions typically (1) starts with the Incident report. Any action committed by a YAHSHUAn should be documented by the Manager through the Incident report and should be email to HR. (2) Once the IRF is received, HR will issue the Notice to Explain to the involved YAHSHUAn. (3) The YAHSHUAn will respond to the NTE within 5 days (holiday/restday included. (4) HR will call for a hearing with everybody involved: the apellant with his/her witness (if there's any), the manager and Ma'am Juvy. (5) After the hearing, the decision will be presented to the apellant and will be documented in the email.

YP Admin Access

YP Administrative Access and Permissions

YP Admin Access provides authorized personnel with elevated permissions to manage and oversee various aspects of our systems.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is YP Admin Access?

YP Admin Access is a set of elevated permissions granted to specific personnel to manage and oversee various administrative functions within our systems.

How can I request YP Admin Access?

To request YP Admin Access, submit a formal request through the IT Help Desk portal. Your request will be reviewed by the IT security team and your department head for approval.

What responsibilities come with YP Admin Access?

With YP Admin Access comes increased responsibility. You must adhere to strict security protocols, maintain confidentiality, and use the access only for authorized purposes. Regular audits are conducted to ensure proper use.

How often is YP Admin Access reviewed?

YP Admin Access is reviewed quarterly to ensure that only personnel who require this level of access retain it. You may be asked to justify your continued need for admin access during these reviews.
